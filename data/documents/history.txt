History is the study of past events, societies, and civilizations. It examines political, economic, social, and cultural developments over time. 
By understanding history, humans learn about successes, failures, conflicts, and innovations that have shaped the modern world. 

Important periods include ancient civilizations, the Middle Ages, the Renaissance, the Industrial Revolution, and the modern era. 
History also studies revolutions, wars, migrations, and technological advancements. Knowledge of history informs current decisions, helps preserve cultural heritage, 
and provides context for understanding contemporary society.
