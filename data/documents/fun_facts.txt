Honey never spoils, and archaeologists have found 3000-year-old honey that is still edible.  
Water can boil and freeze at the same time under special conditions called the triple point.  
Sharks existed before trees.  
Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not.  
The human body contains enough iron to make a small nail.  
Octopuses have three hearts and can change color to blend with their surroundings.  
The longest recorded flight of a chicken is 13 seconds.  
These facts demonstrate the surprising wonders of nature, biology, and physics.
